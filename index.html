Come See Our Rides in Action

25 Hours in Frog Hollow

Saturday - Sunday, November 7-8, 2020

After being cooped up for most of the spring and staying socially distant for much of the summer, we are packing our bikes and taking off to a two day event in the desert. Well, Dave is staying behind to mind the shop. Thanks, Dave! We might be biting off more than we can chew with this one, but we all just built new rides, and we are stoked to try them out on some pretty rough terrain!

8th Annual Santa Fun Ride

Saturday, December 12

This event is going to be a lot different this year. Instead of our yearly bike parade, we are encouraging area cyclists to don their most festive Santa suits and enjoy their favorite neighborhood rides. Keep your eyes peeled for Santa sightings and snap a shot of any cycling Santas. Upload the image to any of our social media platforms, and we will hold a vote on the best Santa suit and the best photo. More information coming soon.

Winter Showcase

Friday - Sunday, January 8-10, 2021

This event is currently under review to incorporate virtual events for social distancing purposes.

Since we don't get out on the trails as much in the winter, we look forward to the Winter Showcase every year as a chance to share our experiences of the rides we had in the summer and fall, plan the first rides of spring, and most of all, help you design the customizations you'd like to make to your bike in the upcoming year. We'll have a lot of new gear to browse through in the store, but you can also special order items online. And don't forget, this is a perfect time to put those gift cards Santa brought you to good use!
